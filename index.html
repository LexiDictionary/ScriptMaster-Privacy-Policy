Overview

Script Master is an educational app that respects your privacy. We collect minimal data and focus on providing a secure learning experience.

What We Collect

All learning content, progress, and settings are stored locally on your device. This data never leaves your device unless you share it.

TTS processing happens locally using your device's built-in speech engine. We don't send any audio data to servers.
